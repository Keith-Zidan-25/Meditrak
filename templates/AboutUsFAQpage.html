General Questions

What should I do if I have a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, dial your local emergency number immediately, typically 102 in India.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our clinic during business hours or using our online appointment booking system.

Medical Conditions

What is diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body regulates blood sugar (glucose).

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

Common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. However, symptoms may vary from person to person.

Appointment Related

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment by contacting our clinic at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you accept walk-in appointments?

While we encourage appointments to ensure timely service, we do accept walk-in appointments based on availability.
